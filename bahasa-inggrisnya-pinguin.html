2. Karakteristik pinguin bahasa Inggris


Penguins are sea-birds in the family Spheniscidae. They use their wings to ‘fly’ underwater, but they cannot fly in the air. They eat fish and other sea food. Penguins lay their eggs and raise their babies on land.
Penguins live only in the Southern Hemisphere of the world: Antarctica, New Zealand, southern Australia, South Africa and South America. The furthest north they get is the Galapagos Islands
Penguins love to swim in the ice cold ocean water. They can swim very fast and can leap out of the water and dive deep looking for food. A layer of fat together with a layer of air keeps penguins warm in the cold water and almost any weather.
All penguins have a white belly and a dark (mostly black) back. Penguins cannot fly, but they can swim very well. Their wings have become stiff and small swimming flippers. They have good hearing and can see underwater. The white and black colors are countershading to help them hide when they swim.
When a predator looking from underwater sees the white belly and wings of the penguin, they can not see it well with the light coming from above. Seen from above, the penguin’s black back blends in with the dark water. Penguins have webbed feet used for paddling in the water. They cannot walk well, so they waddle.
The biggest penguins may stand nearly 110 cm and can weigh almost 40 kg. The smallest kinds are only about 32 cm tall. Penguins have a thick layer of blubber that helps them keep warm, and their feathers are very tightly packed to make another cover. They also have a layer of woolly down under the feathers that are coated with a type of oil that makes them waterproof.

Jawaban:

Penguins (order Sphenisciformes, family Spheniscidae) are a group of aquatic flightless birds. They live almost exclusively in the Southern Hemisphere, with only one species, the Galápagos penguin, found north of the equator. Highly adapted for life in the water, penguins have countershaded dark and white plumage and flippers for swimming. Most penguins feed on krill, fish, squid and other forms of sea life which they catch while swimming underwater. They spend roughly half of their lives on land and the other half in the sea.

17. Ciri-ciri umum dan khusus hewan pinguin dalam bahasa Inggris dan artinya


Kelas: VII
Mata Pelajaran: Bahasa Inggris
Materi: Writing
Kata kunci: Pinguins


Pembahasan:

 

Bahasa Inggris:

 

Pinguins are birds which are well adapted to live in cold polar environment. A penguin has a large head, short neck, and elongated body.

 

The penguin's body is adapted for swimming and pinguin can swim fast in the water to catch fish, its main diet. Its body is streamlined, enabling better movement under water.

 

The legs are set far back on the body, which gives penguins their upright posture on land. Penguin’s feets are webbed, which help to navigate while swimming in the sea.

 

All adult penguins are dark on the dorsal (back) surface and white on the ventral (front) surface. The dark back side absorb heat from sunlight. It also helps pinguin blends in with the dark ocean depths when viewed from above.

 

The light front side blends in with the lighter surface of the sea when viewed from below. The result is that predators or prey are harder to spot penguin between its environment.

 

Terjemahan:

 

Pinguins adalah burung yang beradaptasi untuk tinggal di lingkungan kutub yang dingin. Penguin memiliki kepala besar, leher pendek, dan badan memanjang.

 

Tubuh penguin beradaptasi untuk berenang dan pinguin bisa berenang cepat di air untuk menangkap ikan, makanan utamanya. Tubuh pinguin ramping, memungkinkan gerakan yang lebih baik di bawah air.

 

Kaki-kaki berada di jauh di bagian belakang tubuh, yang memberi penguin postur tegak saat di darat. kaki Penguin berselaput, yang membantu saat berenang di laut.

 

Semua penguin dewasa berwarna gelap pada sisi punggung (belakang) dan putih di sisi dada (depan). Sisi belakang yang gelap menyerap panas dari sinar matahari. Ini juga membantu pinguin membaur dengan kedalaman laut yang gelap bila dilihat dari atas.

 

Sisi depan yang terang menyatu dengan permukaan laut yang lebih terang bila dilihat dari bawah. Hasilnya adalah predator atau mangsa lebih sulit untuk melihat penguin di antara lingkungannya.
