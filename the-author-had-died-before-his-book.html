11. Ubah ke indirect , The artist Said that hE had shown his painting collection The year Before


Jawaban:

Direct / Indirect Speech:

Indirect Speech: The artist Said that he had shown his painting collection The year Before.

Direct Speech: The artist said, "I showed my painting collection last year."

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan bentuk Direct Speech (kalimat langsung) yang diucapkan oleh artis tersebut pada soal dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Kalimat Indirect Speech pada soal menggunakan backshift (pergeseran waktu ke belakang) Past Perfect Tense maka artinya bentuk kalimat Direct Speech (kalimat langsung) nya menggunakan Simple Past Tense.

Simple Past Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object + Time Signal (last year)

Semoga membantu ya.
