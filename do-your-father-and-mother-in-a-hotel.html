8. Kalau mother and father pakau does atau do?


Jawaban:

Jika konteksnya disini menyebutkan mother dan father secara bersamaan maka pakai do karena bentuknya plural. Jika konteksnya disini masing-masing (father/mother) maka pakai does karena bentuknya singular.

Penjelasan:

Do and does

The main definition of “do” is “to accomplish an action.” The main definition of “does” is “a reference to the accomplishment of another.” Both words mean basically the same thing, because “does” is the third person singular present tense version of “do.” The difference is in how each word gets used.

Difference between Do and Does :

“Do” is used for plural (jamak) subjects like “you,” “we,” “they,” “these,” “those,” or “John and I,” as well as with “I.”

Example : They don't like sharks

“Does” is used for singular (tunggal) subjects like “he,” “she,” “it,” “this,” “that,” or “John.”

Example : She doesn't like sharks

Another example : Mother and father do the cleaning in my house. (plural)

My mother does my work. (singular)

My father doesn't like fish. (singular)

source : https://grammar.yourdictionary.com/vs/using-do-vs-does-properly-in-questions-and-sentences.html

My Parent's Job

Pada halaman 129-130 Buku Bahasa Inggris kelas 7, siswa diminta untuk mempelajari contoh pada tabel. Kemudian, siswa diminta untuk berputar mengelilingi kelas bertanya kepada minimal 5 temannya tentang apa pekerjaan orang tuanya dan apa yang dikerjakan orang tua.

Untuk menanyakan pekerjaan orang tua, kita dapat menggunakan kalimat tanya seperti:

1. Hello Beni, what is your father job?/ what is your mother job?

2. Hello Beni, what does your father do?/ what does your mother do?

3. Hello Beni, what is your father?/ what is your mother?

Untuk menjawab pertanyaan tentang pekerjaan orang tua, kita dapat menggunakan kalimat seperti:

1. My father's job is a/an ...... He .......

2. My father is a/an ...... He .......

3. My mother's job is a/an ...... She ......

4. My mother is a/an ..... She .....

Pembahasan

Disini saya memberikan contoh cara mengisi tabel tersebut dengan pekerjaan orangtua teman. Anda bisa memodifikasi atau merubahnya nanti.

Pembahasan Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang dilakukan secara berulang, kebiasaan, rutin dan fakta umum.

Rumus Present Tense (+) S + Verb-1 (-s/es) + O/C (-) S + do/does + not + Verb-1 + O/C(?) Do/does + S + Verb-1 + O/C?

Catatan

Jika Subjek He/She/it/Satu nama

kalimat positive menggunakan Verb+s/es atau HASkalimat negative dan interrogative menggunakan does dan Verb-1 have

Jika Subjek I/You/We/They/Dua nama

kalimat positive menggunakan Verb1 tanpa tambahan -s/es atau HAVEkalimat negative dan interrogative menggunakan do dan Verb-1 have
