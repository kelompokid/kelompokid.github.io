4. (+) my mom ... many vegetables in the market yesterday(-) my mother ... many vegetables at the market yesterday(?) ... my mom buy many vegetables in the market yesterday?â€‹


Halo

Simple Past tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menerangkan sesuatu yang sudah lampau.

Rumus Simple Past:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O?

Jadi,

(+) My mom bought many vegetables in the market yesterday

(-) My mom didn't buy many vegetables at the market yesterday

(?) Did my mom buy many vegetables at the market yesterday?

Penjelasan:

Soal-soal diatas menggunakan bentuk simple past tense yaitu tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan aktivitas yang dilakukan pada masa lampau dan sudah selesai.

Ciri-ciri dari kalimat past tense diatas adalah digunakannya keterangan waktu seperti yesterday, two weeks ago, last night, last Sunday.

Bentuk dari simple past tense adalah:

(+) Subject + verb 2(-) Subject + did not + infinitive without to(?) Did + Subject + infinitive without to

contoh dalam kalimat:

(+) Dinda walked to school by herself yesterday.

(-) Â Dinda didn't walk to school by herself yesterday.

(?) Did Dinda walk to school by herself yesterday?

(+) John slept on the floor last night.

(-) John did not sleep on the floor last night.

(?) Did John sleep on the floor last night?

Dalam past tense, verb yang digunakan adalah verb-2, perubahan verb dari verb-1 ke verb-2, dapat dibedakan menjadi dua yaitu:

Regular verb jika perubahannya dengan menambahkan -d/ed pada verb-1, misalnya: walk-walked, dream-dreamed, listen-listened, smile-smiled.Irregular verb, jika perubahannya dari verb 1 ke verb 2 tidak sesuai pattern diatas (tidak beraturan), misalnya: sleep-slept, write-wrote, read-read, ride-rode, eat-ate, draw-drew.
