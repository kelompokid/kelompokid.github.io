22. alat alat listrik berikut, daya listrik solder,tv,kipas angin sesuai gambar adalah


t= 2jam = 7200 sekon
8jam = 28800 sekon
10jam = 36000 sekon


P = W/t

solder:
W = 2 × 15 × 2 × 7200 = 432000
P = 432000/7200 = 60 watt

tv:
W = 5 × 2 × 5 × 28800 = 1440000
P = 1440000/28800 = 50 watt

kipas angin:
W = 3 × 5 × 3 × 36000 = 1620000
P = 1620000/36000 = 45 watt
