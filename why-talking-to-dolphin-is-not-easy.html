8. Why is the sea eagle easy to recognise?


because of:
1. the size. Their heads and beaks are large.

2. The wings are broad all the way along their length, with obvious primary feathers (fingers) at the end.

3. The bird has a short-tailed and short-necked appearance.

4. The eagles are often mobbed by other birds when they are flying and even a Raven will look tiny next to it.

5. They don’t flap their wings much and even in a strong wind will not be buffeted as much as other birds.

6. A pale head and white tail on adult birds is obvious in good light, while juveniles are all dark and shaggy.sea eagle are easy to recognize from white feathers on the head, chest, and tail. As the name implies, this eagle eats fish and small mammals.
