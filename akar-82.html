8. 13. Diketahui a dan ß merupakan akar-akar persamaan kuadratx2 - 12x - 5 = 0, nilai dari a2 +82 + 3aß =...​


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

x² - 12x - 5 = 0 ➡ a = 1, b = -12, c = -5

α + β = -b/a = -(-12)/1 = 12

α . β = c/a = -5/1 = -5

α² + β²

= (α + β)² - 2(α . β)

= 12² - 2(-5)

= 144 + 10

= 154

Maka, nilai dari α² + β² + 3αβ adalah

α² + β² + 3αβ

= 154 + 3(-5)

= 154 - 15

= 139

[tex]\sf {a}^{b} = a \times a \times a \times a \times ... \times a \times a = ... [/tex]

[tex]\sf {a}^{b} \times {a}^{c} = {a}^{(b+c)}[/tex]

[tex]\sf {a}^{b} \div {a}^{c} = {a}^{(b-c)}[/tex]

[tex]\sf {(a}^{b}{)}^{c} = {a}^{(b \times c)}[/tex]

[tex]\sf {a}^{0} = 1[/tex]

[tex]\sf ({a}^{m} \times {a}^{n}{)}^{b} = {a}^{mb} \times {a}^{nb}[/tex]

[tex]\sf {a}^{m} \times {c}^{m} = (a \times b{)}^{m}[/tex]
