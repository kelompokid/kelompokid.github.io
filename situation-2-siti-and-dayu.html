Pada bagian reflecting ini, siswa diminta untuk mempelajari dialog kemudian mengisi blank dialog sesuai dengan konteks dialog tersebut. Kemudian siswa diminta untuk mendiskusikannya bersama teman dan guru di kelas.

Situation 1

Lisa : Beni, do you have a pet?

Beni  : Yes, I do. I have a dog.

Lisa : I love dogs! What is it like?

Beni : It’s a he. He has curly brown fur and big round eyes. He is very playful.

Lisa  : Wow! I can imagine how adorable he is.

Situation 2

Siti  : Dayu, do you often go to the park near your house?

Dayu : Yes, I do.

Siti  : What is it like?

Dayu : It’s beautiful. There are so many beautiful flowers and big trees.

Siti  : That’s great! We should go there together some time.

Situation 3

Udin : Do you know Farhan Ramadhan?

Dayu : Yes, I do.

Udin : What is he like?

Dayu : He is kind and helpful.  

Situation 4

Lisa : Siti, you look so happy today.

Siti  : Yes, My mother bought me a new cell phone yesterday.

Lisa : May I see it?

Siti  : I want to focus on study at school, so I left it at home.  

Lisa  : What does it look like?

Siti  : It has big screen and the color is pink.
