3 . Prepare ice shavings with salt, put on a bowl.

4 . Then put mixed coconut milk with shredded coconut pulp into the bowl.

How to Make Coconut Water

1. Gather the ingredients. You will need 1 young coconut, a sharp knife, and a spoon.

2. Cut the top off the coconut. Use a sharp knife to cut off the top of the coconut. Make sure to cut off enough of the top so that you can access the coconut water inside.

3. Pour out the coconut water. Use a spoon to scoop out the coconut water from inside the coconut. Be careful not to spill any of the water.

4. Enjoy! The coconut water is now ready to drink. Enjoy it cold or at room temperature.

2.

Procedure for Making Coconut Water

1. Gather the ingredients: one young coconut, a sharp knife, and a bowl.

2. Cut the top off the coconut with the knife.

3. Pour the coconut water into the bowl.

4. Cut the coconut in half and scoop out the flesh with a spoon.

5. Place the coconut flesh in a blender and blend until smooth.

6. Add the blended coconut flesh to the coconut water and stir until combined.

7. Serve chilled or over ice. Enjoy!

14. Resep es kelapa muda jeruk dalam bahasa inggris


Ingredients Coconut Ice Orange

     Young coconut meat
     400 ml soda water fresh / young coconut water
     Meat oranges, cut into pieces
     Ice cubes as needed
     160 ml milk plus citrus flavored syrup

How to Make Coconut Ice Orange

    1. Enter the young coconut meat and 3-4 slices of oranges into serving glasses.
     2. Enter the serving glasses over ice cubes that have filled coconuts and oranges.
     3. Pour the fresh sparkling water into a glass, and also pour 160 ml of orange syrup.
