10. My brother sells two bags? Ubah kebentuk pasive voice


Bentuk passive voice dari kalimat 'My brother sells two bags' adalah The two bags are sold by my brother.

Pembahasan

Kalimat pasif (passive voice) menekankan pada objek yang diberi perlakuan oleh subjek. Kalimat passive ditandai dengan adanya to be + verb 3, kemudian biasanya diikuti dengan kata by.

Pola kalimat passive:

Object + to be (tergantung tense-nya) + verb 3 + by + subject.

Berikut adalah kalimat active dan kalimat passive pada masing-masing tenses.

A. Present Tense

Active : My brother sells two bags.

Passive : The two bags are sold by my brother.

B. Present Perfect Tense

Active : My brother has sold two bags.

Passive : The two bags have been sold by my brother.

C. Present Continous Tense

Active : My brother is selling two bags.

Passive : The two bags are being sold by my brother.

D. Past Tense

Active : My brother sold two bags.

Passive : The two bags were sold by my brother.

E. Past Continous Tense

Active : My brother was selling two bags.

Passive : The two bags were being sold by my brother.

F. Past Perfect Tense

Active : My brother had sold two bags.

Passive : The two bags had been sold by my brother.

G. Future Tense

Active : My brother will sell two bags.

Passive : The two bags will be sold by my brother.

21. 1.She said,"I have seen two bags on the table"2.They said,"We see two bags on the table"3.He said," I ate a bowl of noodle yesterday"4.She said," I am reading a magazine now"​


DIRECT-INDIRECT SPEECH

1) She said (that) she had seen two bags on the table.

2) They said (that) they saw two bags on the table.

3) He said (that) he had eaten a bowl of noodle the day before

4) She said (that) she was reading a magazine at that time.

EXPLANATION

Direct speech adalah ungkapan asli yang disampaikan langsung oleh narasumber. Direct speech terpapar dalam bentuk percakapan dengan disertai tanda petik ganda ("). Sedangkan, reported speech (indirect speech) adalah pernyataan pelaporan dari apa yang telah disampaikan oleh narasumber kepada orang lain tanpa mengubah informasi yg tersirat. Pada umumnya, tanda petik pada direct speech akan dihilangkan.

Perubahan bentuk noun clause verb pada reported speech bergantung pada bentuk reporting verb dan jenis informasi yg disampaikan pada direct speech, apakah berupa informasi faktual, imperative, interrogative dan prohibition.

Jika reporting verb berbentuk present dan future {says, will ask, tells}, noun clause verb tidak mengalami backshift.

Sedangkan, jika reporting verb berbentuk past {said, told, asked}, noun clause verb mengalami backshift :

Verb Tenses

Simple present ➛Simple past

Simple past ➛Past perfect

Present perfect ➛Past perfect

Simple future ➛ Present conditional

Present continuous ➛Past continuous

Past continuous ➛Past perfect continuous

Adverb of time

Now ➛at that time

Today ➛ that day

Yesterday ➛ the day before

Tomorrow ➛ the next day

Last week ➛ the previous week

Yang perlu diperhatikan, dalam merubah direct speech ke indirect speech, yaitu :

1) Jenis reporting verb yg digunakan, berbentuk present atau past.

2) Jenis informasi yg disampaikan, apakah berupa imperative, interrogative, exclamation.

3) Pronoun (kata ganti orang), posisi orang yang terlibat.

4) Verb tense

5) Adverb of time (keterangan waktu)

6) Adverb of place (keterangan tempat/petunjuk)
