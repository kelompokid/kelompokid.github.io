11. penyelesaian turunan 1/ arcsin x


y= 1/arcsin x = sin x
y' = cos xy = arcsin x
x = sin y
d/dx (x) = d/dx (sin y)
1 = cosy . dy/dx
dy/dx = 1/cos y
        = 1/√(1 - sin²y)
         = 1/√(1-x²)

trunan dari 1/arcsin x
= -1 .(arcsin⁻²x) . d/dx (arcsin x)
= -1 / [√(1-x²) . arcsin²x]

23. Y = arcsin( 2x4+5x3) carilah y'


[tex]y=\arcsin{2x^{4}+5x^{3}}[/tex]
[tex]\sin{y}=2x^{4}+5x^{3}[/tex]
[tex]\cos{y} \frac{dy}{dx}=8x^{3}+15x^{2}[/tex]
[tex]\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{8x^{3}+15x^{2}}{\cos{y}}[/tex]

perhatikan bahwa
[tex]\cos{y}=\sqrt{1-\sin^{2}{y}}[/tex]
[tex]\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{8x^{3}+15x^{2}}{{\sqrt{1-\sin^{2}{y}}}}[/tex]
[tex]=\frac{8x^{3}+15x^{2}}{\sqrt{1-(2x^{4}+5x^{3})^{2}}}[/tex]
