I didn't go anywhere last holiday. My mom asked us to stay at home because she didn't get a holiday. I spent most of time playing game online. But I did it after my mother asked me to do the chores. After having breakfast, I washed the clothes and swept the floor. I knew how to get my mom's heart, so I would do the chores and then played game online or watched television. Pembahasan

Dalam menulis suatu cerita yang telah terjadi, kita harus menggunakan past tense. Rumus dari past tense adalah verb 2. Perhatikan kata kerja yang bergaris tebal.

28. Tolong buatkan 1 cerita tentang liburan bahasa inggris menggunakan verb 2..tpi klo bisa ceritanya liburan dirumah gitu ya :) dan artinya :)


Jawaban:

Here's the example of the story:

In the summer holiday of 2016, I had my best holiday at home. Perhaps anyone might wonder how come spending holidays at home can be fun. The answer is only simple in one word, football. Football made it fun as there were two big tournaments happening almost at the same time, followed by the Olympic Games Rio 2016. Those two tournaments were Euro 2016 and Copa America Centenario 2016. Everyday, I could stay at home, read newspaper and books about football, do housework a bit and start watching football matches from around 7 pm till 7 am in the next morning non-stop plus download the highlights afterwards followed by making analysis of the matches. What an exciting holiday!!

The translation: 

Liburan musim panas tahun 2016 adalah yang paling menyenangkan di rumah. Mungkin kalian akan bertanya-tanya bagaimana bisa menghabiskan liburan yang menyenangkan di rumah.
