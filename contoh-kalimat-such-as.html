1. 10 contoh kalimat such as​


Giving Examples using such as

1. My sister is a polyglot. She can speak many languages, such as English, Arabic and Bahasa Indonesia.

2. I love dark colors, such as, black, dark grey and brown.

3. My mom loves collecting fashion stuff, such as bracelet, necklace and bandana.

4. Bella likes to watch live performance, such as concert.

5. He can play some musical instruments, such as, violin and cello.

6. My brother traveled to countries with beautiful buildings, such as Italy, France and Netherland.

7. James prefer healthy beverages such as milk and juice than soda.

8. I'd like to eat Indonesian cuisine such as nasi padang, mie ayam and lontong sayur.

9. Tour objects such as beach and mountain is very popular.

10. GDP in developing countries such as Philippines will continue growing at a high rate.

Pembahasan

Ketika kita ingin membuat suatu kalimat lengkap kita dapat menambahkan frasa such as yang secara umum memiliki arti “contohnya adalah…”

Nah, kata such as aturannya dapat digunakan sebagai frasa di dalam sebuah kalimat.

Contoh:

I can play quite a few musical instruments, such as the guitar, the piano and the cello.Children should avoid eating junk food such as pizza and burger.

2. 10 contoh kalimat yang menggunakan kata such as​


Berikut contoh kalimat dengan menggunakan frasa such as:

Robert enjoys many kind of fruits served at the buffet, such as kiwi, peach, watermelon, and dragon fruit.You should read classics such as Lord of The Rings before you turn 13.My mom love Mexican food, such as burrito, quesadilla and enchiladas.Next year I’d like to visit a place such as Labuan Bajo or Raja Ampat.I visited several cities in Indonesia, such as Pontianak, Makassar and Bandung.Activities such as reading and singing are great for adding your kids' vocabulary.Every day my dad consume foods that are high in Vitamin D, such as  egg yolk and oily fishes.Many countries such as Japan have 4 seasons.The store sells various kind of electronics, such as televisions, washing machines, and refrigerators.Animals such as dolphin are considered as one of the smartest mammals.

Pembahasan

Frasa such as biasanya digunakan sebagai bentuk formal dari like yang berfungsi untuk memberikan contoh mengenai hal yang sedang dibicarakan. Penggunaannya cenderung dipakai dalam tulisan. Fungsi frasa such as sama dengan for example/for instance, namun biasanya for example/for instance dapat diletakkan di awal kalimat sedangkan such as tidak bisa. Semua ekspresi di atas dapat digunakan untuk pertanyaan singkat dalam sebuah percakapan, berikut contohnya:

Chandler: "There are many countries I would like to visit".

Monica: "Such as?" / "Like?" / "For example?" / "For instance?"

3. contoh dialog such as (didalam dialog ada kata such as)


Explanation:

Such as is used to give an example or more of something that we mention before. It can be placed in the middle of a sentence or at the end of a sentence.
For example,
- I like to eat Japanese food, such as sushi, ramen, udon, and mochi (at the end of a sentence)
- Fruits such as orange and strawberry are rich in vitamin c (in the middle of a sentence)
Please note that, if such as is placed in the middle of a sentence, we will not put a comma before and after it, but if it is placed at the end of a sentence, we will put a comma before it. This is necessary for writing. Such as can be replaced by "Like"

Answer:
Here's an example dialogue using such as.

A: Hey, B!
B: Hello, A! What's up?
A: I want to ask something.
B: What is it?
A: What fruit that is rich in vitamin C?
B: People said yellow fruits such as orange and lemon are rich in vitamin C. But, it is not fully correct.
A: Why?
B: I ever read a journal about it. According to the scientist, there are many other fruits such as guava and strawberry which are richer in vitamin C than yellow fruits. Vegetables such as broccoli are also rich in vitamin C.
A: Oh, I see. Thank you, B.
B: Don't mention it.

5. Buatlah dialog 2 orang tentang 'for example' dan 'such as' masing2 lima kalimat? *tolong yakk:))*


Kita sering kali ingin memberikan contoh ketika menggunakan bahasa Inggris. Phrase yang dapat kita gunakan adalah for example atau such as. Kedua phrase ini fungsinya sama tapi dalam cara penggunaannya berbeda.

Pembahasan

Fungsi dari such as dan for example hampir sama, yaitu digunakan untuk menyebutkan contoh. Misalnya ketika sedang membicarakan suatu topik tertentu dan kita ingin memberikan contoh dari topik tersebut. Perhatikan contoh dibawah ini:

We should eat more healthy food, for example salad, fruit, and vegetables..You should do more exercise to make your body healthier, such as jogging, playing football, and swimming.Perbedaan Such as dan For Example

Dalam beberapa kasus terdapat perbedaan dalam penggunaan such as dan for example. Berikut ini perbedaan cara penggunaan such as dan for example:

For example dapat digunakan untuk mengawali kalimat sedangkan such as tidak.For example tidak dapat memotong sebuah klausa sedangkan such as bisa.Dialog Bahasa Inggris Menggunakan Such As dan For Example

Berikut ini adalah contoh dialog bahasa Inggris yang terdapat penggunaan such as dan for example:

Jane: Hai Tom. I have a problem with my body.

Tom: What is it?

Jane: I can lose my weight easily. Do you have any suggestions?

Tom. I think you should stop eating junk food, such as hot dogs, hamburgers, and pizza.

Jane: Yeah. It is very difficult to do that because I like hot dogs and numbers very much.

Tom: You should do you best if you want to lose you weight fast. Then you have to do more exercise, for example playing volleyball, running, and swimming.

Jane: I will try to do more exercise then.

Tom: That isn't enough. You have to start to eat more healthy food, such as vegetables and fruit.

Jane: I see. Okay. I’ll follow your suggestion. Thank you, Tom.

Tom: Anytime, Jane.

8. buat contoh kalimat for example and for instance :isi dimana letak komabuat contoh such as and like:isi dimana letak koma


Jawaban:

1. For example adalah kata yang sering digunakan untuk menujukkan contoh, namun ada beberapa hal yang perlu diperhatikan yaitu:

A. Jika for example terletak di awal kalimat, harus diikuti dengan koma:

There are many activities in the morning. For example, the student goes to school.

B. Jika for example terletak di tengah kalimat, umumnya antara subjek dan predikat :

Social security, for example, is the responsibility of our government.

2. For instance memiliki fungsi yang sama dengan for example. Namun, umumnya for instance lebih sering digunakan di tengah kalimat.

In the economic sector, for instance, Indonesia is still better than Thailand.

3. e.g. juga dapat digunakan untuk memberikan contoh. E.g. sendiri adalah kependekan dari exempli gratia yang merupakan berasal dari bahasa Latin.

High level smartphones, (e.g. Samsung and Iphone) are the leader of the world market

A. Jika tidak berada dalam kurung kita bisa menggunakan koma sebelum e.g.

Today people, e.g. children, play with smartphone.

Orang-orang sering salah dalam penggunaan i.e. yang sering disamakan dengan e.g. Perbedaannya adalah i.e itu sama dengan that is yang umumnya digunakan untuk mendefinisikan seuatu, sedangkan e.g. digunakan untuk memberikan contoh/ sepadan dengan e.g.

Penjelasan:

Ketika kita diharuskan untuk menulis jurnal internasional, surat lamaran pekerjaan dalam Bahasa Inggris, ataupun Surat Resmi dalam Bahasa Inggris, kita harus mempunyai kemampuan menulis yang bagus. Gaya menulis dalam bahasa Inggris memiliki ciri khas sendiri. Salah satunya adalah ketika kita ingin memberikan sebuah contoh dalam tulisan tersebut. Memberikan contoh di dalam bahasa Inggris dapat menggunakan for example, for instance, e.g., such as, like, dan sebagainya.

11. apa perbedaan 'such as' dan 'for example'? dan berikan contohnya!


Jawaban:

The difference between 'such as' and 'for example', which actually has the same meaning, lies on the position in the sentence. Here is the example:

Several notable football managers begin to lose the opportunity to lead big teams. For example, Carlo Ancellotti and Rafael Benitez can only be in the non elite teams this season.Several notable football managers such as Carlo Ancellotti and Rafael Benitez begin to lose the opportunity to lead big teams.

Penjelasan:

The phrase 'for example' and 'such as' have the same meaning, that is to state the examples related to the content in the sentence. Yet, they are quite different in terms of their position in a sentence.

'For example' can be used in the middle of the sentence as well as at the beginning of it. Moreover, it has to be between two commas when it is put in the middle of the sentence. Here is how: The students have less interest in social science, for example, history, geography, sociology. The students have less interest in social science. For example, they tend to be lazy in learning history or geography.

'Such as' can only be used in the middle of the sentence but not at the beginning. Yet, this phrase does not need two commas in between. Here is how:                                      

Several notable football manager such as Carlo Ancellotti and Rafael Benitez begin to lose the opportunity to lead elite teams

Such

Kegunaan such adalah sebagai berikut:

1) Dalam situasi formal, such + noun berarti “tersebut” dan diletakkan sebelum a / an. Namun apabila kata bendanya adalah “Uncountable Nouns”, maka artikel “a/an” tidak digunakan.

I don’t like such an idea.

(Saya tidak suka ide itu)

Such honesty is really needed.

(Kejujuran seperti itu sangat dibutuhkan)

2) Such digunakan sebelum Adjective + Noun.

I do like such beautiful girl.

(Saya sangat menyukai perempuan yang cantik seperti itu)

I agree with such brilliant idea.

(Saya setuju dengan ide brilian itu)

3) Dalam situasi tidak resmi, such dapat juga digunakan untuk memberikan informasi baru, ketika pembicara ingin menekankan apa yang dikatakannya.

He’s such an idiot!

(Dia memang bodoh)

She has such a marvelous voice!

(Suaranya memang luar biasa)
