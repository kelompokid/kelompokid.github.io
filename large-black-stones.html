2. How is a descriptive text constructed?

A descriptive text is constructed with an opening paragraph in first paragraph. There is a topic sentence that introduces the object going to be described. And then a series of paragraphs follow the opening to describe the parts or the features or the specific characteristics of the subject.

3. What are the language features of a descriptive text?

The language features of a descriptive text are

Using adjective and classifiers in nominal group.Using simple present tense.There are many noun phrases in trying to make readers get the mental picture of what is being described.
