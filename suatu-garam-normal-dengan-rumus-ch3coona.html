15. Banyaknya garam ch3coona dalam larutan 100 ml ch3coona dengan pH = 9 adalah blablabla Mr ch3coona = 82 k = 1,8 kali 10 minus 5


HIDROLISIS

CH₃COONa adalah garam basa, sehingga berlaku [OH⁻] = √Kw/Ka [CH₃COO⁻]
pH = 9
pOH = 14 - 9
pOH = 5
pOH = -log [OH⁻]
5 = -log [OH⁻]
[OH⁻] = 10⁻⁵
10⁻⁵ = √10⁻¹⁴ / 10⁻⁵ x [CH₃COO⁻]
10⁻¹⁰ = 10⁻¹⁴ / 10⁻⁵ x [CH₃COO⁻]
10⁻¹⁰ / 10⁻⁹ = [CH₃COO⁻]
10⁻¹ = [CH₃COO⁻]
mol = M x V
mol = 10⁻¹ x 0,1
mol = 10⁻²
Massa = mol x Mr
Massa = 0,01 x 82
Massa = 0,82 gram
∴Banyak garam yang diperlukan adalah 0,82 gram

17. Sebanyak 4,1 g garam CH3COONa dilarutkan dalam air hingga volumenya 250 ml.jika tetapan hidrolisis garam tersebut 2X10^-8 maka pH larutan CH3COONa sebesar Ar=82


4,1 gram CH3COONa 250 mL
Kh = 2 × 10^-8
Mr CH3COONa = 82

pH ... ?

M = gram/Mr × 1000/mL
M = 4,1/82 × 1000/250
M = 0,05 × 4
M = 0,2

Garam CH3COONa bersifat basa
[OH^-] = √( Kh × Mgaram)
[OH^-] = √( 2 × 10^-8 × 0,2)
[OH^-] = √( 4 × 10^-9)
[OH^-] = 2 × 10^{-4,5}

pOH = - log [OH^-]
pOH = - log 2 × 10^{-4,5}
pOH = 4,5 - log 2

pH = 14 - pOH
pH = 14 - (4,5 - log 2)
pH = 14 - 4,5 + log 2
pH = 9,5 + log 2

b. 9

Penjelasan:

CH3COONa => CH3COO^- + Na^+

Reaksi hidrolisis:

CH3COO^- + H2O => CH3COOH + OH^-

Na^+ tidak terhidrolisis karena merupakan komponen NaOH yg bersifat basa kuat

valensi = koefisien anion yg terhidrolisis = 1

[OH-] = √( { Kw / Ka } x M garam x valensi )

=> √( { 10^-14 / 10^-5 } x 10^-1 x 1 )

=> 10^-5 M

pOH = - log 10^-5

=> 5

pH = 14 - pOH

=> 14 - 5

=> 9

pH = 9

pOH = 14 - 9 = 5

pOH = - log [OH-]
5 = - log [OH-]
- 5 = log [OH-]
- 5 = [OH-]

[OH-] = 10^-5

Hitung banyaknya Molar garam hidrolisis

[OH-] = √Kw / Ka . M
10^-5 = √10^-14 / 1,8 x 10^-5 . M
10^-5 = √10^-9 / 1,8 . M
(10^-5)² = 10^-9 / 1,8 . M
10^-10 = 10^-9 / 1,8 . M
M = 1,8 . 10^-10 / 10^-9
M = 1,8 . 10^-1
M = 0,18 mol/liter

M = a / Mr x 1000/V
0,18 = a / 82 x 1000/100
14,76 = a x 10

         = 5
