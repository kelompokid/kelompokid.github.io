Perjalanan kami di Maluku tengah membawa kami pada satu tempat di sekitar kaki gunung Binaiya. Dari jalan Trans Seram, kami berkendara dengan mobil sekitar 3 kilometer masuk daerah hutan yang sangat sepi dengan jalan yang penuh dengan kerikil-kerikil tajam siap menghadang, panas terik matahari terasa merasuk dalam pori-pori kulit, mata kami mulai lelah dengan sinarnya, hingga kami mendapatkan sang mentari tertutup awan. Indonesia only consists of tribes and ethnic groups at the forefront. These tribes have a diversity that is second to none, are very authentic and have local wisdom in their life identity. Our journey in Central Maluku took us to a place around the foot of Mount Binaiya. According to information, the school building has indeed been built by the government, but the village is short of teachers willing to teach.
