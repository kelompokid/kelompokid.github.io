12. buktikan bahwa tan 345 derajat = (akar 3-2)


Kelas 10 Matematika
Bab Trigonometri

tan 345°
= tan (315° + 30°)
= (tan 315° + tan 30°)/(1 - tan 315° . tan 30°)
= (-1 + 1/3 . √3) / (1 - (-1) . 1/3 . √3)
= ((-3 + √3)/3) / ((3 + √3)/3)
= (-3 + √3)/(3 + √3) . ((3 - √3)/(3 - √3))
= (-9 + 3√3 + 3√3 - 3) / (9 - 3)
= (-12 + 6√3)/6
= -2 + √3
= √3 - 2
