#deret aritmetika
hari 1 = 45 menit
hari 2 = 60 menit
hari 3 = 75 menit
....
....

lama latihan dalam seminggu:
S7 = 7/2 × (2 . 45 + 6 . 15)
= 7/2 × (90 + 90)
= 630 menit
= 10 jam 30 menit

Permasalahan aritmatika
U2 = 60
b = 15
a = 60 - 15 = 45
n = 7
Un = a + (n-1)b
U7 = 45 + 6 x 15
U7 = 45 + 90
U7 = 135 m
Sn = n/2 ( a + Un)
= 7/2( 45 + 135)
= 7/2 (180)
= 630 menit
atau
630/60
= 10 30/60 menit
--> 10 jam 30 menit
