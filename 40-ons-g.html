Dalam satuan ukuran berat atau massa, antara lain :

gr (gram)
dg (desigram)

Setiap turun satu tingkat dikali 10 dan setiap naik satu tingkat dibagi 10.

1 kg = 10 hg
1 hg = 10 dag
1 dag = 10 gr
1 gr = 10 dg
1 dg = 10 cg
1 cg = 10 mg
1 ton = 1000 kg
1 ton = 10 kuintal
1 kuintal = 100 kg
1 kg = 2 pon
1 pon = 5 ons
1 hg = 1 ons
1 kg = 10 ons
1 ons = 100 gr

Mari kita lihat soal tersebut.
1 kg = 10 hg
⇔ 3 kg = 3 x 10 = 30 hg.
⇔ 8 kg = 8 x 10 = 80 hg.
⇔ 20 kg = 20 x 10 = 200 hg.

1 kg = 10 ons
⇔ 4 kg = 4 x 10 = 40 ons.
⇔ 5 kg = 5 x 10 = 50 ons.

1 kg = 10 x 10 x 10 = 1.000 gr
⇔ 5 kg = 5 x 1.000 = 5.000 gr.

1 hg = 10 x 10 = 100 gr
⇔ 7 hg = 7 x 100 = 700 gr.
⇔ 6 hg = 6 x 100 = 600 gr.

1 ons = 100 gr
⇔ 40 ons = 40 x 100 = 4.000 gr.
⇔ 9 ons = 9 x 100 = 900 gr.
