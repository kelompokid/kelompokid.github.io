Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

- flour at first, 5026 g

- then, bought another 4157 g

- transferred some flour in the bag so that flour in the bag = twice as much as in the packet.

To be asked :

amount of flour left in the packet = . . . . ?

Answer :

amount of flour in the bag = twice amount of flour in the packet

by making the algebra equation :

x + 2x = total

x + 2x = 5026 + 4157

3x = 9183

x = 3061

Conclusion :

The flour in the packet = 3061 g
