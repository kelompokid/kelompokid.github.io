25. 4 dm3+1000ml-2000 mm3 = .... a. 30 dm3 b.30cc c.3liter d.3 m3( pake cara )


Converting volume units

4 dm³ = 4× 1000000mm³=4000000mm³
1000ml = 1000 cm³= 1000000 mm³

So,

4dm³ + 1000ml - 2000mm³
= (4000000 + 1000000 - 2000) mm³
= (3000000-2000)mm³
= 2998000 mm³

= 2998cm³
= 2998 cc
= 2998 ml
or
= 2,998 dm³
= 2,998 liter

Your problem has no options
so, the answer is n/a

1 cm³= 1 cc = 1 ml
cc stands for centimeter cubic
