4. Hitunglah hasil pemangkatan dibawah ini mengunakan sifat" Pemangkatan

b. 1/12^3 atau 1/1.728

a. 8^8*8^13 = 8^(8+13) = 8^21 = 9.223.372.036.854.775.808

c. x^15/(x^7*x^9) = x^15/(x^(7+9)) = x^15/x^16 = x^(15-16) = x^-1 = 1/x

d. (15^10*15^5)^4 = (15^(10+5))^4 = (15^15)^4 = 15^(15*4) = 15^60 = 3,6768468716933021540096999600113e+70
