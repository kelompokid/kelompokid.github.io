TKG
• kecepatan rms
T1 = 300 K, v1 = v
T2 = __?, v2 = ½v

Kecepatan rms
v = √(3kT/m)
v sebanding dengan akar T
v2 / v1 = √T2 / √T1
½v / v = √(T2/300)
½ = √(T2/300)
¼ = T2/300
T2 = 300/4 = 75 K = -198 °C ← jwb

∆T = T1 - T2
∆T = 300 - 75 = 225 K = 225 °C ← jwb
