1. Plzz bantu kak aku mohon ... someone knock at the door last night? * a.Did b.Do c.Was d.Does


DID someone knock at the door last night?

Jawabannya A. Did √

PEMBAHASAN Past Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang kejadiannya terjadi di masa lampau.

Waktu lampau:

=> yesterday

=> Last night ✔️

=> two weeks ago

Rumus Past Tense (+) S + V2(-) S + did not + V1 (?) DID + S + V1 ?

Catatan Pada kalimat Positive menggunakan Verb-2Pada kalimat Negative (-) dan kalimat tanya (?) menggunakan Verb-1 karena sudah ada AUXILIARY PAST yaitu DID

KESIMPULAN

DID someone knock at the door last night?

Keterangan:Soal dalam bentuk kalimat tanyaDan ada waktu lampau “Last night”

Maka pilihan ganda yg tepat adalah

a.Did => Auxiliary Past ✔️

b.Do => Auxiliary Present

c.Was => Be Past

d.Does => Auxiliary Present

27. someone knock at the doorlast night?O wereO wasO didO doWe ... to the railway station tocatch the train this morning.o hurryO hurriedO hurriesO hurrying​


Jawaban:

1. was someone Knock the door last night

2. we hurried to the railway station to catch the train this morning

Jawaban:

1. did

2. hurried

Penjelasan:

1. ... someone knock at the door last night?

last ... (Misal: last night, last month) --> adverb of time Simple Past tense.

knock --> V1, berarti kata tanya pakai modal, tidak mungkin to be. Modal dalam pas tense yang ada di pilihan jawaban adalah did.

2. this morning --> adverb of time Simple Past tense.

Hurry --> V2 = hurried.

Simple Past tense --> menyatakan kegiatan yang terjadi dan sudah selesai di masa lampau.

Rumus:

(+) S + V2 + O.

(-) S + did + not + V1 + O.

(?) Did + S + V1 + O?

Yes, S + did.No, S + didn't.

(QW) Question words + did + S + V1 + O/adverb?

*) Question words: who, what, where, when, why, how, which, etc.

Kalimat nominal: kalimat yang predikatnya bukan verb/kata kerja.

Rumus: S + to be (was/were) + O.

was --> S: I, he, she, it, singular/tunggal.were --> S: you, they, we, plural/jamak.
