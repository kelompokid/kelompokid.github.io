Simple Past vs Present PerfectA. Simple Past Tense

Simple Past Tense biasanya digunakan untuk menyatakan tindakan di masa lalu, dan telah selesai di masa lalu.

Penanda waktu yang digunakan: yesterday, last week, last month, two days ago, this morning.

Bentuk simple past

    (+) Subject + verb 2  

    (-) Subject + did + NOT + verb 1  

    (?) Did + subject + verb 1 .... ?

Contoh:  

She slept too late last night.I didn't see him yesterday.Did you take my umbrella?What did you do last weekend?When someone knocked the door, I was studying.Where were you last night?I didn't see him this morning.Last month, I bought a new car.B. Present Perfect Tense

Present Perfect Tense biasanya digunakan untuk menyatakan aktivitas yang baru saja terjadi, telah dilakukan beberapa waktu yang lalu hingga tepat sebelum berbicara. Tense ini juga dapat digunakan untuk menyatakan pengalaman.

Penanda waktu yang digunakan: since, yet, already, never, ever, recently, lately, for.

Bentuk present perfect

    (+) Subject + has/have + Verb 3

     (-) Subject + has/have+ NOT + Verb 3

     (?) Has/Have+ subject + verb 3 .... ?

Contoh:  

She has just finished doing homework.I haven't eaten, yet.She has just arrived.Have you ironed the clothes?Have you ever been to London?What have you done?I have never seen such a big butterfly.

Remember THE RULE!! Gunakan 'have' untuk subject I, you, they, we. (ayudewi^^) Gunakan 'has' untuk subject he, she, it.


PembahasanComplete the following sentences with the suitable words in the brackets!

1. Dr. Nakamata's 48th birthday has come and (go) gone and he is still inventing.

  Explanation: kesetaraan dalam kalimat. "..has come and gone" sama-sama present perfect.

2. Brooke Martin, now 16, (enable) has enabled her invention with Wifi, so it can connect to the internet.

  Explanation: kejadian tersebut masih berlangsung sampai saat ini, sehingga 'it can connect to internet', present tenses.

3. Over the years, kids (come up) has come up with some pretty cool inventions.

  Explanation:  'over the years', terjadi beberapa tahun lalu hingga sekarang adalah penanda waktu untuk present perfect.  

4. By embedding protein into windows, Nikita Rafikov (find) found a way to light homes without the use of electricity.

  Explanation:  aktivitas penemuan di masa lalu, simple past.

5. Between 1873 and 1974, Bell (spend) spent long days and nights trying to protect the harmonic telegraph.

  Explanation:  Penanda waktu 'between 1873 and 1974' menunjukkan aktivitas di masa lalu, simple past.

6. In 2012, a thirteen-year-old girl (launch) launched a potentially lucrative business built around her unusual cure for hiccups.

  Explanation:  Penanda waktu 'in 2012, menunjukkan aktivitas di masa lalu, simple past.

7. The language of Braille (become) has become the tactile writing system used by the blind and the visually impaired around the world.

  Explanation:  "Bahasa braile telah menjadi sistem penulisan taktil yang digunakan oleh orang buta dan yang rusak penglihatannya di seluruh dunia". Situasi tersebut terjadi sampai saat ini. Since tidak dipakai karena akan membuat kalimat tidak utuh.

8. Martin Cooper, the inventor of cell phone, (not, think) didn't think that in about 35 years, more than half the people on Earth would have cellular telephones.

  Explanation: pemikiran di masa lalu sebelum akhirnya Martin Cooper mengetahui bahwa separuh penduduk bumi menggunakan telepon, simple past.

9. In 1685, Johann Zahn (propose) proposed a design for the first handed reflex camera. Ahead of this time, it would take another 150 years before his invention became a reality.

  Explanation: telah jelas simple past dengan penanda waktu 'In 1685'.

10. Anang Tadar's innovation won him the Dinanath Pandey Smart Idea Innovation Award in March this year, and according to reports, UNICEF (express) has expressed interest in refining his prototype in order to make it ready for the market.

  Explanation: Perhatikan 'in order to make it ready for the market', ini menunjukkan aktivitas tersebut berlangsung sampai saat ini, present perfect.

Simple Present Tense:

Nominal: (+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adjective / Noun / Adverb

(-) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adjective / Noun / Adverb

(?) Tobe (is/am/are) + Subject + Adjective / Noun / Adverb + ?

Verbal: (+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

(-) Subject + Do/Does + not + Verb-1 + Object

(?) Do/Does + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + ?

(?) 5W1H (What) + Do/Does + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + ?
