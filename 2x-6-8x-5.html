[tex]\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt[3]{8x^6+2x^2+5} }{x^2+5}=\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt[3]{8x^6+2x^2+5} }{x^2+5}\times\frac{1/x^2}{1/x^2}[/tex]
