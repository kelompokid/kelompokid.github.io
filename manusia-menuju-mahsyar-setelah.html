25. Setelah dibangkitkan manusia akan berada di Padang mahsyar Padang Mahsyar merupakan tempat


Padang Mahsyar adalah tempat yang sangat luas di mana seluruh manusia dikumpulkan setelah mereka dibangkitkan dari kubur atau tempat lain yang Allah kehendaki untuk diadili satu per satu di hadapan Allah subhanu wa ta'ala untuk ditimbang semua amalnya dan dihisab.

Answer:

>>> Islamic Lesson

Padang Mahsyar is a place in Islamic religious beliefs which is believed to be a place for gathering people after the resurrection on the Day of Judgment. At that time, all humans will be summoned to face Allah SWT in Padang Mahsyar and be held accountable for all the actions that have been committed while living in the world. In the hadiths and verses of the Qur'an, it is explained that in Padang Mahsyar, humans will be shown their deeds and all the good and bad things they have done in the world are counted.

Explain: After that, they will be decided by Allah SWT, whether they will go to heaven or hell.
