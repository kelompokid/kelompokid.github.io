6. What is useful as a support and storage of food reserves in plants is​


Jawaban:

Roots are mostly underground structures present in plants that develop from the radicle. They absorb water, minerals and nutrients from the soil. They also anchor plants into the ground and give them support and prevent them from blowing away by the wind or washing away by the soil. A modified lateral root that functions as a storage organ is called tuberous root or storage root. Carbohydrate is the stored food reserve. For example, carrots and beets are tuberous roots that are modified from taproots.

22. Aunt is watering plants di ubah dalam kalimat present continuous tense


Rumus Present Continuous Tense-nya =

[negative form (-)] Subject + is/am/are + not + Verb-ing + Object

JAWABANNYA: "Aunt is not watering plants"

[interrogative form (?)] Is/Am/Are + Subject + Verb-ing + Object

JAWABANNYA: "Is Aunt watering plants?"

25. 1. use the broom to ..... the floorA. sweep B. swept C. sweeping D. sweeps2. A watering can is useful to .... the plants A. fertilizeB. plantsC. growD. water3. the bucket can ..... a lot of water it is a big bucketA. hold B. heldC. holding D. to hold tolonglah dikerjakan please besok disuruh kumpul


Penggunaan To + Infinitive

1. Use the broom to A. sweep the floor

2. A watering can is useful to D. water the plants

3. The bucket can A. hold a lot of water. It is a big bucket.

Pembahasan

To infinitive adalah kata kerja yang dibentuk dengan menambahkan to sebelum kata kerja.

To infinitive berfungsi sebagai kata benda, kata sifat, dan kata keterangan.

Contoh :

To Infinitive sebagai kata benda

To forgive (subject) is not as easy as you think

I want to punch (direct object) her

To Infinitive sebagai kata kerja

It is the best class to admire

He brings a laptop to use in exam

To Infinitive sebagai kata keterangan

To finish the online exam, she needs to submit the answers

Ting must drink the rat poison to die

Sedangkan kalimat yang menggunakan modal (can, will, should, must) tidak diikuti to infinitive sebelum kata kerja.

Contoh :

She can accomplish the job thoroughly

He must come on time

She should tell the truth earlier
