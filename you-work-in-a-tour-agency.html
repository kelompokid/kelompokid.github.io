21. you work in a tour agency. you see a young gantleman enter your office awkwardly. offer your service and try to convince him to take one of your holiday packagesbuatkan percakapan bahasa inggris sesuai dengan situasi diaatas ( write a dialog for each of the following situations)


Pembahasan:


Sebelum membuat dialog, sebaiknya kita mengetahui kalimat seperti apa yang biasanya digunakan untuk menawarkan (offering) dan bagaimana merespon suatu offering (responding an offer).


1. Expression of offering

Expression of offering merupakan suatu ekspresi yang digunakan untuk menawarkan suatu benda atau bantuan.

Berikut contoh expression of offering:

Expression of offering help:

- May I help you?

- Can I help you?

- What can I do for you?

- Would you like me to ...?

- Can I lend you a hand with that?


Expression of offering things:

- Would you like some ...?

- Would you like something to ...?

- Should I get you ...?

- What can I get for you?

- How about ... ?


2. Responding an offer

Untuk merespon sebuah tawaran kita dapat menerima atau menolaknya. berikut adalah ekspresi yang dapat digunakan untuk menerima dan menolak tawaran.

Expression of accepting an offer:

- Yes, Please.

- Thanks, I'd love to.

- That sounds nice.

- Thank you, I appreciate it.


Expression of refusing an offer:

- No, Thanks.

- Thank you, but I can do it myself.

- I appreciate it, but I can do it myself.

- Not for me, thanks.

- It's alright I can do it myself, thanks.


Berikut contoh dialog sesuai dengan situasi diatas:

You: Good morning, sir. What can i help you?

the young gentleman: Is this XXX tour agent?

You: You're at the right place. This is XXX tour agent. May i hep you?

the young gentleman: Yes, please. I want to have a holiday abroad.

You: Have a seat first, sir. So, when and where will you have the holiday, sir?

the young gentleman: On June or July and i want to visit a country within Asia. I think Hong Kong is good.

You: yes, Hong Kong is a good place for shopping. Hong Kong also has a lot of tourist attraction such as Hong Kong Disney Land and Temple Street Night Market. But, how about taking our holiday package sir?

the young gentleman: Can you explain me the holiday package?

You: Sure. We have a holiday package which are suitable with you request. But unfortunately, the destination is not Hong Kong.

the young gentleman: You can explain it first, then i'll consider about it.

You: alright, sir. We have two holiday packages. The first package is a vacation in Singapore for 5 days. The price is 5 million rupiahs per person. The price includes the flight ticket, hotel, transportation, universal studio and gardens by the bay entrance ticket. The second package is a vacation in Thailand for 5 days. The price is 4,5 million rupiahs per person. The price includes the flight ticket, hotel, transportation, translator, various tourist attractions entrance ticket. I would suggest you to take the second package, sir because the price is cheaper and also Thailand is well-known as a heaven for shopaholics and also has a lot of interesting tourism objects. Comparing to Hong Kong, the price of fashion stuff and other things in Thailand is much cheaper but the quality is similar. The tourism objects are also wonderful.

a young gentleman: I'll take the second package if that so.

You: Alright. Please, go to the reservation section to make a reservation.

a young gentleman: Alright.

You: Thank you for trusting us to take care of your holiday, sir.
