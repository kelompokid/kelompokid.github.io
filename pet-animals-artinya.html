20. Tuliskan lah dalam bahasa Inggris masing masing 3 binatang dari wlid animals , domestik animals , pet animals tentang deskripsi ny Tolong kak jawab kan pliss


Jawaban:

WILD ANIMAL

- LION The lion is an animal species of the felidae family or cat type. Lions are animals that live in groups. Usually consists of one male and many females. This group guards the territory under their control.

- EAGLE The eagle is warm-blooded, has wings and a body covered with midrib hair. As birds, eagles reproduce by laying eggs that have a hard shell in the nest they make. He looks after his son until he is able to fly. Eagles are predatory animals.

- CROCODILE Crocodiles are large reptiles that live in water. Scientifically, crocodiles include all species of members of the Crocodylidae family, including the spit crocodile (Tomistoma schlegelii). ... Crocodiles are ancient animals, which have changed little due to evolution since the time of the dinosaurs.

DOMESTIC ANIMAL

- COW Cows are the livestock of the Bovidae tribe and the children of the Bovinae tribe. ... Cows are raised primarily for use as milk and meat for human food. The by-products such as skin, offal, horns and feces are also used for various human needs.

- GOAT Goats are ruminant animals that have a four-part stomach consisting of the rumen, reticulum, omasum, and abomasum. Like other ruminant mammals, goats are even-fingered animals. Female goats have breasts with two nipples, compared to cows with four nipples.

- PIG The pig is a kind of ungulate animal with a long snout and a clay-nosed nose and is an animal originally from Eurasia. Pigs are omnivores, which means they eat both meat and plants.

PET ANIMAL

- CAT Cats are a kind of carnivore. The word "cat" usually refers to cats that have been domesticated, but can also refer to "big cats" such as lions, tigers and tigers. Cats have been associated with human life since 6,000 years BC, from the cat skeleton on the island of Cyprus. The ancient Egyptians from 3,500 BC used cats to keep rats or other rodents away from barns where crops were stored.

- DOG Dogs are predators and scavengers, have sharp teeth and strong jaws to attack, bite, and tear food. ... When compared to the bony structure of the human foot, dogs technically walk on tiptoe on their toes.

- RABBIT Rabbits are mammals from the Leporidae family, which can be found in many parts of the earth. Rabbits reproduce by means of a child called vivipar. Formerly, this animal was a wild animal that lived in Africa to mainland Europe. ... Today a number of types of rabbits are used as pets and meat animals.
