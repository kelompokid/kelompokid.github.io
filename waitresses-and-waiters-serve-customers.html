13. change the active to passive 1. shakespeare wrote that play. 2. waiters and waitresses serve the costumer. 3. tom will open the door. 4. alex is preparing the report. 5. shirley has suggested a new idea.


Jawaban:

1. That play was written by Shakespeare.

2. The costumer is served by waiters and waitresses.

3. The door will be opened by Tom.

4. The report is being repaired by Alex.

5. A new idea has been suggested by Shirley.

Penjelasan:

1. Passive simple past tense: object + was/were + verb 3 + by subject.

2. Passive simple present tense: object + am/is/are + verb 3 + by subject.

3. Passive present future tense: object + will be + verb 3 + by subject.

4. Passive present continuous tense: object + am/is/are + being + verb 3 + by subject.

5. Passive present perfect tense: object + has/have + been + verb 3 + by subject.

27. Change the active voice become passive voice1. Shakespeare wrote that play.2. Alex is preparing that report.3. Waitresses and waiters serve customers.4. John cooks noodle5. You have opened the window.​


Jawaban:

1. Shakespeare wrote that play.

That play was written by Shakespeare.

2. Alex is preparing that report.

That report is being prepared by Alex.

3. Waitresses and waiters serve customers.

Customers are served by waitresses and waiters

4. John cooks noodle.

Noodle is cooked by John.

5. You have opened the window.

The window has been opened by you.

Penjelasan:

Untuk mengubah kalimat aktif menjadi kalimat pasif, yang harus diperhatikan adalah tense dan kata kerja yang digunakan. Pola active voice dan passive voice dalam bahasa Inggris sama seperti kalimat aktif dan kalimat pasif dalam bahasa Indonesia.

Passive voice, secara umum berbentuk:

Object + auxiliary verb + {been/being] + verb 3 + by subject.
