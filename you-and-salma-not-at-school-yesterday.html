1. 13. You and Salma ... not at schoolyesterday.A.isB. amC. wasd.were​


Jawab: D. Were

Pembahasan:

13. You and Salma ... not at school yesterday.

= Kamu dan Salma ... tidak di Sekolah kemarin

A. is = To Be utk subject singular Present

B. am = To Be utk subject I

C. was = To Be utk subject singular Past Tense

d. were​ = To Be utk subject Plural Past Tense

Alasannya memilih Jawaban D

* Karena ada keterangan waktu “yesterday” (kemarin)

* subject jamak (you and salma)

Maka gunakan Past Tense

(-) S + was/were + not + Adj/Adv/Noun

= You and Salma were not at school yesterday.

6. She did not......to school yesterday


Jawaban :

She did not go to school yesterday. (Dia tidak pergi ke sekolah kemarin)

PEMBAHASAN

Karena auxiliary verb yang digunakan pada kalimat negatif nya adalah "did" yang menunjukan bahwa kalimat di atas bentuk simple past, maka kata kerja yang digunakan pada kalimat negatif nya harus Verb-1.

Simple past adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan kejadian yang terjadi pada masa lampau. Rumus nya :

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + Did + not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O

Contoh :

(+) She went to school yesterday.

(-) She did not go to school yesterday.

(?) Did she go to school yesterday?

18. she did not...to school yesterday​


Jawabannya adalah go

PEMBAHASANSimple Past Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan di masa lalu atau Peristiwa yang terjadi di masa lampau.

Ciri waktu lampau

=> yesterday (kemarin).✔️

=> Last night (tadi malam)

=> two days ago (dua hari lalu)

Rumus Past Tense

(+) S + Verb-2 + Complement

(-) S + did not + Verb-1 + Complement

(?) Did + S + Verb-1 + Complement

KESIMPULAN

➡️ She did not go to school yesterday

Dia (perempuan) tidak pergi ke sekolah kemarin

Keterangan:Subject ➡️ SheNegative Form➡️ did notVerb-1 ➡️ go ✔️Complement ➡️to school yesterday
