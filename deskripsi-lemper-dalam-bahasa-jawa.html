Berikut jawaban soal-soal di atas:

Iya, saat ini lemper bisa dibungkus selain dengan daun pisang. Alasannya bisa dijumpai dalam teks yang menyebut lemper kini ada yang dibungkus dengan bahan plastik. Pemilihan plastik bisa jadi untuk kepraktisan.Tidak, isi lemper saat ini biasanya berupa abon sapi ataupun daging ayam. Lemper biasanya dijual oleh pedagang jajanan pasar. Terkadang lemper juga bisa dijumpai pada toko kue tradisional dan modern. Lemper hadir dalam beragam variasi baik cara masak maupun isian. Meski begitu, bahan utama lemper tetaplah beras ketan.

12. cari kalimat passive voice halaman 147 bahasa Inggris kelas 9 ( lemper )​


Jawaban:

The passive voice sentences are

Lemper is made of a handful lump of steamed glutinuous rice. It is filled with chicken or beef filling. Each piece is wrapped in a banana leaf, and the wrapping is secured on both ends with tooth picks or lidi. Lemper in the banana leaf is steamed, or cooked over a very hot barbeque or grill. If the glutinuous rice is well-cooked before lemper is filled and wrapped, it can be served straight away with the banana leaf still green and fresh​.

Penjelasan:

Ada dua jenis tense yang digunakan pada teks Lemper di atas yaitu

Passive simple present tense: object + am/is/are + verb 3.

Contohnya adalah is made, is filled, is wrapped, dan lain-lain.

Passive modals: object + modals + be + verb 3.

Contohnya adalah can be served.

13. Cara membuat lemper dalam bahasa Inggris


Jawaban:

How to make lemper ayam

1.Ingredients

250 grams of glutinous rice

125 ml Santan (medium)

salt to taste

1 sheet of Pandan leaves

to taste Lemongrass

to taste Salam Leaves

100 gram Boneless, boiled, shredded chicken breast

1 piece of Laos / galangal

2 Spring onions

1 clove Garlic

1/2 teaspoon Coriander

to taste Sugar sand

to taste Smooth pepper

banana leaf to taste

2 candlenuts

2. Steps:

* First, Wash the sticky rice and then soak it in cold water for about 10 minutes. Drain well. Then steam for about 15-20 minutes, remove from heat and set aside.

* second, Boil coconut milk with enough salt and pandan leaves. Turn off the stove. Add sticky rice, stir until coconut milk is completely absorbed by the sticky rice. Steamed sticky rice until cooked (approximately 15-20 minutes).

*Puree onion, garlic, coriander, hazelnut. Saute with a little oil until fragrant. Add bay leaves, laos, lemongrass. Stir well.

*Add boiled chicken that has been quipped, add the remaining salt, pepper and granulated sugar (add water if necessary). Cook until all the ingredients are mixed, the water and seasonings are absorbed. Lift, set aside.

*Take enough sticky rice (about 35-40 grams). Flat, fill with chicken mixture, Oval shape until the contents are not visible.

*Wrap it in a banana leaf. And now, you can eat lemper.
