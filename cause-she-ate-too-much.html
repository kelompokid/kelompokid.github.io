29. Exercise Make the Sentence of Cause and Effect 1. Cause: She ate too much Effect: She got stomachache 2. Cause: Early man learnt to grow food Effect: their lives became easier 3. Cause: I ran out of money Effect: I borrowed money from my brother​


Jawaban:

1) - She got stomach-ache because She ate too much
- She ate too much, that's why She got stomach-ache
- Because She ate too much, She got stomach-ache
- She ate too much causing Her Stomach-Ache
- In a cost of eating too much, Stomach-Ache got Her.

2) - Early man learnt to grow food, that's why their lives become easier
- Early man learnt to grow food, and as a result, their lives became easier.
- Early man learnt to grow food resulting in their lives became easier
-
3) -I borrowed money from my brother because I ran out of money.
- The reason I borrowed money from my brother is because I ran out of money
- I ran out of money, that's why I borrowed money from my brother.

Penjelasan:

Terdapat banyak variasi kalimat dengan penghubung yang berbeda namun berujung memiliki Arti yang sama. Untuk penggunaan Cause dan Effect biasanya dapat ditandai dengan kosa-kata berikut:

- Because, Cause, Causing- That's why- In a cost, cost- as a result, result, resulting
