13. Which statement used past tense and which statement used present perfect???


Past Tense

1. You performed very well at the storytelling contest.

2. When i was at elementary school.

3. I joined storytelling contest in district level.

4. But, it wasn't English storytelling contest.

5. I enjoyed the contest and became the first winner.

6. I got an experience to join an English speech contest.

7. It was held by the education board of my district.

8. I became the first runner up, but i was very happy at the time.

- Past tense ditandai dengan penggunaan kata kerja kedua (verb two).

- Past tense juga ditandai dengan adanya was atau were sebagai to-be untuk kalimat dengan adjective, noun, ataupun adverb.

Present Perfect

1. Have you ever joined similar contests before?

2. Have you ever joined a writing contest?

Present perfect ditandai dengan adanya have sebagai to-be dan diikuti dengan kata kerja ketiga/verb three/past participle
