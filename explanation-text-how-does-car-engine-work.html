PEMBAHASAN Pertanyaan pada soal:

How does your father go to work?

WH questions => HowAuxiliary => DoesSubject => your fatherVerb-1 => goComplement => to workInterrogative => ?

Berdasarkan keterangan di atas:Kalimat bentuk tanya (?) karena menggunakan WH questions (How)Kalimat bentuk Present Tense karena menggunakan Auxiliary DOESMaka jawabannya juga harus dalam bentuk Present Tense

Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg dilakukan secara berulang/rutin, kebiasaan, dan hal2 bersifat umum atau fakta.

Rumus Present Tense (+) S + Verb-1 (-s/es)(-) S + Do/Does + not + Verb-1?(?) WH + Do/Does + S + Verb-1?

Catatan

Jika Subjek tunggal:

kalimat positive (+) menggunakan Verb+s/eskalimat negative (-) dan interrogative (?) menggunakan Auxiliary DOES

Jika Subjek jamak:

kalimat positive (+) menggunakan Verb1 tanpa tambahan -s/eskalimat negative (-) dan interrogative (?) menggunakan Auxiliary DO

KESIMPULAN

(?) WH + Do/Does + S + V1?

=> How does your father go to work?

Bagaimana ayahmu pergi kerja?

(+) S + Verb-1 (-s/es)

=> he goes to work by car

Dia pergi kerja dengan mobilMenggunakan Verb+es (go+es) karena subject tunggal (he)

10. explanation text about how a tsunami happens


The term of “tsunami” comes from the Japanese which means harbour (“tsu”) and wave (“nami”). A tsunami is a series of waves generated when water in a lake or a sea is rapidly displaced on a massive scale.

A tsunami can be generated when the sea floor abruptly deforms and vertically displaces the overlying water. Such large vertical movements of the earth’s crust can occur at plate boundaries.

Subduction of earthquakes are particularly effective in generating tsunami, and occur where denser oceanic plates slip under continental plates.

As the displaced water mass moves under the influence of gravity to regain its equilibrium, it radiates across the ocean like ripples on a pond.

Tsunami always bring great damage. Most of the damage is caused by the huge mass of water behind the initial wave front, as the height of the sea keeps rising fast and floods powerfully into the coastal area.


Generic Structure of Explanation Text about How Tsunami Happens
We have said above that explanation text are organized in general statement and sequenced explanation. After reading that short example, then we will see the text organization below!
1. Paragraph of General Statement: It is the paragraph which tell the reader what about the text is. Mostly the topics are in general categories.

A tsunami is a series of waves generated when water in a lake or a sea is rapidly displaced on a massive scale

2. Paragraphs of Sequenced Explanation: This is the writer explanation in detail on how the thing stated in the first paragraph happens

A tsunami can be generated when the sea floor abruptly deforms and vertically displaces the overlying water. Subduction of earthquakes are particularly effective in generating tsunami radiates across the ocean like ripples on a pond. Tsunami always bring great damage.

21. B. How many types of explanation text?c. How many Structure of an explanationText ?​


Explanation Text

b. How many types of explanation text?

There are two types of explanation which focus on “How” and “Why”.

“How”

How does a pump work?How does a computer work?How are mountains formed?How does a spider spin a web?

“Why”

Why do some things float or sink?Why is the ozone layer getting thinner?Why does iron go rusty?Why do living things need food?

c. How many structure of an explanation text?

Structure of explanation text consist of 3 parts:

Statement - explaining overall phenomenon in general (menjelaskan tentang fenomena yang terjadi secara garis besar/umum).Sequence explanation - explanation about the phenomenon in sequence from the first step to the end (penjelasan tentang fenomena secara runtut dari awal ke tahap selanjutnya).Conclusion (optional) - conclusion of the all the explanation (kesimpulan dari penjelasan keseluruhan).

Pembahasan

Explanation text adalah sebuah bacaan yang menjelaskan tentang bagaimana suatu hal dapat terjadi/bekerja. Biasanya teks ini digunakan dalam bidang seni atau ilmu pengetahuan.

Fungsi sosial dari explanation text adalah untuk menjelaskan alasan bagaimana dan mengapa suatu hal dapat terjadi. Pada teks tersebut, alasan-alasan tersebut diuraikan lebih lanjut dan lebih mendalam.

Tujuan explanation text:

Menguraikan sebuah proses yang biasanya berkaitan tentang fenomena alam, sosial, sains dan kebudayaan.Menjelaskan bagaimana dan mengapa fenomena tersebut terjadi.

Ciri kebahasaan explanation text:

Menggunakan simple present tenseMenggunakan abstract nounMenggunakan passive voiceMenggunakan action verbs
